All transactions undergo 256-bit Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption protocols. Your data is stored on sandboxed localized systems (`localStorage` during prototype review) and is isolated from third-party networks.

3. Cookies & Tracking

You maintain absolute sovereignty over your profile. You may edit your data in your profile settings dashboard at any time or request complete erasure of your transaction histories by contacting our vault desk.
